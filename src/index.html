About

.Wav Pool, a Los Angeles based music licensing company, was created in 2018 by a four person team with an immeasruable amount of music experience. The company's focus, centers around helping musicians create sustainable careers through music licensing for ads, films, and online content.

Our artists are some of the most eclectic, inspired, ground breaking and experienced. They offer you new, transformative audio aesthetics for your project. Whether the music sounds cinematic, nostalgic or futuristic, it comes from artists who have dedicated their life to their art. As a result, it's always original.

All of our music is cleared, and ready to sync. Just send us an email with the details of your project and we'll take care of the rest. In addition, all of our music is easily downloadable through our catalog in high quality format. To request login credentials to access our catalog, or for information on rates, please email: info (at) wav-pool.com
